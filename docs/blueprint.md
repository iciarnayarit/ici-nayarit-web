# **App Name**: YouVersion Reimagined

## Core Features:

- Daily Bible Verse: Display a relevant Bible verse each day. User may click on 'Share' or 'Subscribe' options.
- Reading Plans & Devotionals: Provide a selection of reading plans and devotionals to browse and follow.
- Multi-Platform Access: Link to apps in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft burgundy (#800020), evoking a sense of warmth and heritage.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F5F5F5), creating a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted violet (#800080), used sparingly for highlights and calls to action, to evoke the tradition.
- Font pairing: 'Alegreya' (serif) for headlines to convey tradition and sophistication, paired with 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text to ensure readability.
- Clean, organized layout with clear sections for verses, reading plans, and calls to action.
- Simple, elegant icons to represent different sections and features.
- Subtle animations to transition between sections and highlight content.
